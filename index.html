Project Overview
The SafeStep solves the problem of monitoring the physical safety of elders or people facing cognitive burdens. This target audience faces a shortage of personnel in the home caretaker industry by monitoring the safety of the user from environmental factors. The SafeStep solves this shortage by being a compact, affordable product that takes the burden off of already strained caretakers. This system allows for constant monitoring of the user while being introduced as a wearable shoe that do not degrade the quality of life for the user. It solves the problem of having to remember to put on certain monitoring devices; the shoes are designed for daily use.
